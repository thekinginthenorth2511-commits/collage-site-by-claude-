About KLE BCA Nipani

Two decades of academic excellence and innovation

Our Story

Shaping Tech Leaders Since 2001

KLE Society's BCA College Nipani is a premier institution affiliated to Karnataka University, Dharwad. Established under the prestigious KLE Society umbrella, we have been nurturing digital talent for over two decades.

Located in the heart of Nipani, our campus blends academic rigour with a vibrant student culture, producing graduates who lead in software development, data science, and entrepreneurship.
